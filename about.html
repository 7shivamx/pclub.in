Programming Club (or PClub) is a community of students highly enthusiastic about coding, ethical hacking, Web Designing and all other aspects of programming. We are a team currently involving a group of 3 coordinators (pre-final year) and 17 secretaries (sophomores). Facebook group of PClub (https://www.facebook.com/groups/pclubiitk/) has grown to a massive size of 18K members as an active Q and A forum. We are some of the most passionate, talented and earnest group of people in the campus.
We organizing activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests, hackathons, Capture The Flag contests, et all. Lecture series are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things.
We also develop various applications to be used by the campus junta; like Puppy Love (during valentines week) and organize activities like Linux Install fest.
During summers, We offer a variety of projects to the first year students. It acts as a great Our members have regularly excelled at competitions like ICPC world finals, Techfest (IITK won all the programming competitions at Techfest 2013), etc. IOPC, the algorithmic programming contest at Techkriti is one of the biggest programming competitions in India.

Feel free to contact any of our members any time in case of any queries! We hang out on IRC at #pclub
